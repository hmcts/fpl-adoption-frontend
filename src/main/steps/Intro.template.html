You can apply to adopt a child placed in your care through:

  • placement by consent
  • a placement order

You can only apply if the child:

  • has lived with you for at least 10 weeks in England or Wales - there are different rules for Northern Ireland and Scotland
  • is under 18 at the time you apply
  • has never been married or in a civil partnership

You must have lived in the British Isles for at least 12 months before you apply.

You should talk to your adoption agency or social worker before you apply. This will help you decide if it's the right time for the child, you and your family.

How to apply

It costs £170 to apply.

You can apply to adopt 1 or more child in your care in the same application. It will be £170 for each application, not for each child.

You'll need to provide each child's:

  • birth certificate – you'll need to upload a scan and post the original
  • placement order details – contact your social worker or adoption agency if you're not sure
  • birth parents' names and, if known, addresses

You'll need to provide each applicant's:

  • birth certificate
  • photo ID – 2 pieces for each, for example driver's licence and passport photo page
